# Privacy Policy

Socapi uses the Pinterest API to let users connect their Pinterest account and manage Pinterest content.

## Data we access

After a user authorizes the app, Socapi may access:

- Pinterest account information
- Pinterest boards
- Pinterest pins

## How we use data

We use this data only to provide app functionality, such as reading boards, reading pins, creating boards, and publishing pins.

We do not sell user data.

## Data sharing

We do not share Pinterest user data with third parties except as required to operate the app.

## User control

Users can disconnect the app from their Pinterest account at any time through Pinterest settings.
